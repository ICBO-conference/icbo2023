About Program

  • Conference Dates: August 28th to September 1st (Monday-Friday)
    • Workshops and tutorials: August 28th - 29th, 2023
    • Main conference: August 30th - September 1st, 2023

Workshops

  • W1: Ontologies for Infectious and Immune-mediated Disease Data Science (Full-day)
    Co-organizers: Asiyah Yu Lin, Alexander Diehl, John Beverley, and Lindsay Cowell
    Hybrid workshop (both in-person and virtual participation) - In english
  • W2: The 7th International Cells in Experimental Life Science Workshop, CELLS 2023 (Half-day)
    Co-organizers: Alexander D. Diehl and Yongqun "Oliver" He
    Hybrid workshop (both in-person and virtual participation) - In english
  • W3: 12th Vaccine and Drug Ontology Studies (VDOS) 2023 Workshop (Half-day)
    Co-organizers: Junguk Hur, Cui Tao, and Yongqun "Oliver" He
    Virtual workshop with Transmission to In-Person Participants - In english - PDF Presentation
  • W4: FOHTI-23 (FAIR Ontology Harmonization and Trust Data Interoperability) (Half-day)
    Are ontologies really FAIR? Exploring the challenges and opportunity for improvement
    Co-organizers: Anna Maria Masci and Asiyah Yu Lin
    Virtual workshop with Transmission to In-Person Participants - In english

Tutorials

  • T1: Natural Language Processing Tutorial for Biomedical Text Mining (Half-day)
    Co-organizers: Senay Kafkas, Sumyyah Toonsi, Sakhaa Alsaedi
    Virtual Tutorial with Transmission to In-Person Participants - In english
  • T2: OBO Foundry Tutorial: Introduction to workflows and best practices for ontology development (Half-day)
    Co-organizers: Nicole Vasilevsky, Nico Matentzoglu and Tiago Lubiana
    Hybrid tutorial (both in-person and virtual participation) - In english
    Tutorial agenda.
  • T3: BFO as a top-level ontology for information systems modeling (Full-day)
    Co-organizers: Maurício B. Almeida, Jeanne L. Emygdio and Fernanda Farinelli
    Hybrid tutorial (both in-person and virtual participation) - In portuguese
  • T4: Machine Learning with Ontologies (Half-day)
    Co-organizers: Maxat Kulmanov, Robert Hoehndorf, Sarah Alghamdi, Azza Althagafi, Sumyyah Toonsi, Fernando Zhapa-Camacho
    Hybrid tutorial (both in-person and virtual participation) - In english
  • T5: Ontology-Driven Conceptual Modeling with UFO, gUFO, and OntoUML (Half-day)
    Organizer: Giancarlo Guizzardi
    Hybrid tutorial (both in-person and virtual participation) - In portuguese
